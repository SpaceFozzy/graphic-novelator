And still the pale phosphorescense glowed in that detestably ancient woodwork. God! how old the house was! Most of it built before 1700. A feeble scratching on the floor downstairs now sounded distinctly, and Ammi's grip tightened on a heavy stick he had picked up in the attic for some purpose.
